Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the venue?
Either by car or taxi. Address and GPS coordinates are right above. The ceremony, cocktail, lunch and party all take place in the venue, at Quinta de Reguengos.
What time should I arrive?
We're asking everyone to arrive at 12:30. The ceremony will start at 13:00.
I'm taking my car, where should I park?
The venue has a parking space (free of charge).
What should I wear?
Whatever makes you comfortable. Something elegant but casual. It will be a perfect spring day. During the night, you may need a jacket or something warmer.
Does your wedding have a theme?
Not yet, but regardless of what we choose it will not influence what you want to wear.
What kind of food will be served?
Traditional Portuguese food. There will be enough variety for all tastes, so we hope.
Will there be dancing?
Yes, as much as you can take it!
What time will the reception end?
It's up to you :-)
Is there a gifts registry?
Not really. However, if you wish, you can make a donation to help this lovely couple getting started with their life together - and recover from the wedding :P. Drop us an email to dulceandfred@gmail.com
I am coming from out of town. Where should I stay?
We recommend Lisbon, Cascais or Sintra. If you need help with selecting an hotel let's know. For booking accommodations you can try either Airbnb, Hoteis.com or Booking.com.
Can I bring a date with me?
Yes. Please let's know by the RSVP deadline.
When is the RSVP deadline?
April 13th, so that we can communicate to the venue and have fun choosing the tables layout :)
I have a question not answered here; how do I contact you?
